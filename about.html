About Us

SASTA Amazon was founded on 30 January, 2024 by Suyash N from his garage in Pune, Maharashtra. The company initially was an online marketplace for books, but incrementally expanded into a multitude of product categories, a strategy that has earned it the moniker "The Everything Store". SASTA Amazon.com, Inc. is an Indian multinational technology company focusing on e-commerce, cloud computing, online advertising, digital streaming, and artificial intelligence
